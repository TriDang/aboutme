About Me

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City, and I'm currently a lecturer in the Department of Engineering, School of Science, Engineering & Technology (SSET), at RMIT University, Vietnam. Before joining RMIT, I had been working at HCMUT from 2009 to 2020 as a lecturer, at the Vietnamese-German University (VGU) from 2016 to 2018 as a visiting lecturer, and at Hoa Sen University from 2004 to 2007 as an instructor. Additionally, I have spent time working in the industry as a Project Leader (Forix), Technical Project Manager (Seldat), and Technical Leader (Raksul).

At RMIT, I'm responsible for coordinating several courses such as Algorithms & Analysis, Database Applications, Full Stack Development, and Further Programming. I'm also the head coach of the RMIT ICPC team, delivering competitive programming training to talented students. Recently, I've set up a research group focusing on the security and privacy issues of AI/ML algorithms and how human users interact with AI/ML systems.
